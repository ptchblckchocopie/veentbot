# Account and Getting Started

## Creating an Account

To use Veent Tix, you need to create an account:
1. Visit v2.veent.io/sign-in
2. Follow the registration process
3. Provide your full name, email address, and other required details
4. Verify your account through the confirmation email

During registration, you must provide complete, accurate, and truthful information. You are solely responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

## Account Security

### Password Safety
- Do not share your password with others
- Do not use another user's account without authorization
- Use a strong, unique password for your Veent Tix account

### If Your Account is Compromised
If you suspect any unauthorized access or account compromise:
1. Immediately notify Veent's support team at support@veenttix.com
2. Change your password as soon as possible
3. Review your recent activity and ticket purchases

## Account Obligations

As a Veent Tix user, you agree to:
- Provide accurate information during registration and purchases
- Keep your account credentials secure
- Not engage in fraudulent, deceptive, or abusive activity
- Not resell, duplicate, or counterfeit tickets
- Not upload malicious software or harmful code
- Not impersonate any person or misrepresent your affiliation
- Not circumvent or attempt to bypass payment or security systems
- Not use the platform for spam, phishing, or fraudulent activity

## Account Suspension

Veent reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts found engaging in:
- Ticket scalping
- Fake event listings
- Misuse of payment systems
- Fraudulent or deceptive activity
- Any violation of the Terms and Conditions

In the event of termination, any pending tickets or access rights associated with your account may be forfeited, and no refunds will be issued.

## For Event Organizers

### Getting Started as an Organizer
If you're an event organizer interested in using Veent Tix:
1. Visit www.veent.io/contacts
2. Fill out the inquiry form with your details:
   - First and Last Name
   - Email address
   - Company Name
   - Phone Number (optional)
   - Type of event (Concert, Fun Run, Festival, Conference, Workshop, Private Gathering, or Other)
   - Details about your inquiry
3. The Veent Tix team will contact you to discuss next steps

### Organizer Responsibilities
As an event organizer on Veent Tix, you are fully responsible for:
- All aspects of your events (content, pricing, scheduling, promotions)
- Compliance with local laws and regulations
- Ensuring event listings contain truthful, non-misleading information
- Ensuring the event proceeds as advertised
- Handling any disputes with ticket holders
- Processing refunds if you choose to cancel an event

Veent Tix merely provides the digital infrastructure for ticket sales and event visibility. Veent does not guarantee ticket sales or event attendance.

## Browsing Events

You can browse upcoming events without an account:
- Homepage: www.veent.io — see recommended events
- Discover page: www.veent.io/discover — browse all upcoming events sorted by date
- Individual event pages: Each event has its own page (on *.veent.net) with full details, ticket options, and pricing
