# Tickets and Payments Guide

## How to Buy Tickets

To buy tickets on Veent Tix:
1. Visit www.veent.io/discover to browse upcoming events
2. Select the event you're interested in
3. Choose your ticket type and quantity
4. Complete your purchase through the secure payment system
5. You'll need a Veent Tix account to complete the purchase

You can also find events on the homepage at www.veent.io under "Recommended Events."

## Ticket Types

Events on Veent Tix can have multiple ticket tiers. Common ticket types include:
- **VVIP** — Premium experience with the best seats/access
- **VIP** — Enhanced experience with priority seating
- **Platinum** — Premium general admission
- **Premium** — Standard premium access
- **Lower Balcony** — Seated area in the lower section
- **Upper Balcony** — Seated area in the upper section

Some ticket types support seat selection where you can choose your specific seat. Ticket prices are set by the event organizer and vary per event.

## Receiving Your Tickets

Once you've completed your purchase, your tickets will be delivered automatically:
- **Email delivery**: Sent to your registered email address with a digital ticket containing a QR code
- **Account access**: Available through your Veent Tix account under "My Tickets"

You may present your digital ticket (QR code) at the event entrance, or if preferred, print a physical copy. Please ensure your email and account details are accurate before completing your order to avoid delivery issues.

## What to Bring to the Event

When attending an event, you need:
- A valid copy of your ticket — either digital (QR code on your phone) or a printed physical copy
- A government-issued photo identification card (ID) that matches the name on the ticket (may be required by some events)

## Lost Tickets

You can access your tickets anytime through your Veent Tix account dashboard under "My Tickets." Veent is not responsible for lost, deleted, stolen, or damaged tickets once delivered. Keep your digital ticket accessible on your device or print a backup copy.

## Accepted Payment Methods

Veent Tix accepts the following payment methods:
- **Maya** — Digital wallet payments
- **Visa** — Credit and debit card payments
- **GCash** — Mobile wallet payments
- **Bank Transfer** — Direct bank payments

All payments are processed securely through trusted payment partners with enterprise-grade encryption.

## Payment Security

Your payment information is protected by:
- SSL encryption for all transactions
- Firewalls and secure cloud hosting
- Industry-leading encryption standards
- Role-based access controls
- Regular system updates and security audits

Veent Tix uses enterprise-grade security to ensure your attendee's data and payments are protected at all times.

## Ticket Prices and Fees

Ticket prices, seating arrangements (if applicable), and event descriptions are determined and published by the respective event Organizer. Veent Tix acts as a payment facilitator — the contractual relationship for the event lies directly between you and the Organizer.

## Important: All Sales Are Final

All ticket purchases made through Veent Tix are considered final, binding, and strictly non-refundable. Once a transaction has been completed and a confirmation has been issued, no refunds, reversals, or exchanges shall be granted for any reason whatsoever.
