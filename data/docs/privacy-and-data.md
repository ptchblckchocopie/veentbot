# Privacy and Data Protection

## Overview

At Veent Tix, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and the guidelines of the National Privacy Commission (NPC).

This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website, create an account, or purchase tickets through our platform.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information (voluntarily provided)
- Full name
- Email address
- Contact number
- Payment information (processed securely through trusted payment partners)
- Event preferences or purchase history

### Non-Personal Data (automatically collected)
- Browser type, device information, and IP address
- Usage data for analytics and service improvement

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Process ticket orders and payments
- Send your e-tickets and transaction updates
- Notify you about upcoming events and exclusive offers (if you opt in)
- Improve our website's performance and user experience
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information.

However, we may share limited data with:
- **Event organizers** — to verify ticket purchases or manage access at events
- **Payment partners** — for secure transaction processing (Maya, Visa, GCash, Bank Transfer)
- **Service providers** — for hosting, analytics, and email communication

All third parties are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

## Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored securely using encrypted databases and restricted access. We keep your information only for as long as needed to:
- Fulfill the purpose for which it was collected
- Comply with legal or accounting obligations

After that, your data will be safely deleted or anonymized.

## Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data — request to see what information we hold about you
- **Correct** your personal data — request corrections to inaccurate information
- **Delete** your personal data — request removal of your information from our systems
- **Withdraw consent** at any time — opt out of marketing communications or data processing
- **File a complaint** with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) if you believe your rights have been violated

## How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer:
- Email: privacy@veenttix.com
- Include your full name and registered email address for identity verification
- We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe

## Security Measures

We use industry-standard security measures such as:
- SSL encryption
- Firewalls and secure cloud hosting
- Role-based access controls
- Regular system updates and audits

These help protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

## Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on our website at www.veent.io/data-privacy with a new effective date.

## Children's Privacy

Veent Tix is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have it removed.
