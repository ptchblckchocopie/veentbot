# Website Navigation Guide

## Main Website: www.veent.io

### Top Navigation Bar
The main navigation bar appears on every page and has the following links:
- **Veent logo** (top left) — links to the homepage at www.veent.io
- **Explore Events** — links to the discover page at www.veent.io/discover where you can browse all upcoming events
- **Contacts** — links to the contact form at www.veent.io/contacts for event organizers
- **LOGIN** — links to the sign-in page at v2.veent.io/sign-in

### Homepage (www.veent.io)
The homepage has these sections from top to bottom:
1. **Hero Section** — "Where Great Events Begin." with buttons for "Book Your Ticket" (goes to discover page) and "Create Event" (goes to login/sign-in)
2. **Recommended Events** — Shows featured upcoming events with dates. Click any event to go to its event page. Click "Explore for more" to see all events.
3. **Features for Organizers** — Information about Easy Event Setup, Advanced Ticketing, Secure Payments, Real-Time Analytics, and Marketing Tools
4. **Our Partners** — Logos and names of partner organizations
5. **Testimonials** — Reviews from event organizers who use Veent Tix
6. **FAQ Section** — 5 frequently asked questions with expandable accordion answers
7. **Available Payment Methods** — Shows accepted payment methods (Maya, Visa, GCash, Bank Transfer)
8. **Footer** — Quick links, contact info, and legal links

### Footer (appears on every page)
The footer contains:
- **Quick Links**: Discover, FAQ (scrolls to FAQ section on homepage), Contact Us
- **Contact & Legal**: hello@veent.io, Privacy Policy (www.veent.io/data-privacy), Terms & Conditions (www.veent.io/terms-condition)
- **Social Media**: Facebook (facebook.com/veent.io), Instagram (@veent.tix), LinkedIn (linkedin.com/company/veent), TikTok (@veent_tix)

### Discover Page (www.veent.io/discover)
- Shows all events sorted by date (newest first)
- Each event card shows the event name and date
- Click any event to go to its dedicated event page
- Events that have already passed are marked as "ENDED"
- Has a "See More Events" button to load older events
- There is a "Date" sort option at the top

### Contact Page (www.veent.io/contacts)
- **For Event Organizers Only**
- Has a form with fields: First Name, Last Name, Email, Company Name, Phone Number (optional), Event Type (Concert, Fun Run, Festival, Conference, Workshop, Private Gathering, or Other), and Inquiry Details
- Submit the form and the Veent Tix team will contact you

### Sign In / Login Page (v2.veent.io/sign-in)
- Options to sign in: "Continue with Google" or email/password
- Has a "Sign up" link for new accounts
- After signing in, you access your Dashboard and Wallet
- Through the dashboard you can access "My Tickets" to view purchased tickets

### Privacy Policy Page (www.veent.io/data-privacy)
- Full data privacy policy in 7 sections
- Explains what data is collected, how it's used, data sharing, storage, your rights, security measures, and policy updates

### Terms & Conditions Page (www.veent.io/terms-condition)
- Full terms and conditions in 17 sections
- Covers introduction, acceptance of terms, services description, account obligations, ticket purchases, delivery, organizer responsibilities, prohibited uses, refund policy, intellectual property, privacy, user conduct, liability, modifications, termination, governing law, and contact info

## Event Pages (*.veent.net)

Each event has its own dedicated page on a subdomain of veent.net (for example, lanimisaluchacdo.veent.net).

### Event Page Structure
1. **Countdown Timer** — Shows days, hours, minutes, and seconds until the event starts
2. **Event Description** — Full details about the event written by the organizer
3. **Event Details** — Date, time (with timezone), and venue with address
4. **Share Event** button — Share the event on social media
5. **Ticket Selection** — "Book Your Ticket Now" section where you:
   - See all available ticket tiers with prices
   - For events with seat selection (like VVIP, VIP), click "Select Seats" to choose specific seats
   - For general admission tickets, use the + and - buttons to select quantity
   - Click "Next" to proceed to checkout
6. **Need Help? Contact Us** — Shows the organizer's contact information (name, phone, email, or social media)
7. **Hosted by** — Shows who organized the event
8. **Powered by** — Shows "Veent Tix" branding
9. **Verify QR Code** — Link to verify ticket QR codes

### How to Buy Tickets (Step by Step)
1. Find an event on www.veent.io/discover or the homepage
2. Click on the event to go to its event page
3. Scroll down to "Book Your Ticket Now"
4. Choose your ticket type and quantity (or select seats for seat-selection events)
5. Click "Next" to proceed
6. You'll need to log in or create a Veent Tix account
7. Complete payment using Maya, Visa, GCash, or Bank Transfer
8. Receive your digital ticket via email and in your account under "My Tickets"
9. Present your QR code ticket (digital or printed) at the event entrance
