# Refund and Cancellation Policy

## No Refund Policy

All ticket purchases made through Veent Tix are considered final, binding, and strictly non-refundable. Once a transaction has been completed and a confirmation has been issued, no refunds, reversals, or exchanges shall be granted for any reason whatsoever, including but not limited to:

- Personal schedule conflicts
- Travel delays or transportation issues
- Changes of mind
- Illness or health reasons
- Dissatisfaction with the event
- Unforeseen circumstances such as weather disruptions or technical difficulties

By completing a purchase on Veent Tix, you acknowledge and agree that the "No Refund" policy applies universally to all transactions processed through the Platform.

## Why No Refunds?

Veent Tix is a service intermediary. All monetary proceeds (after platform fees) are directly transferred to the event Organizer, who maintains full responsibility for event management. Since funds go directly to organizers, Veent Tix cannot process refunds on their behalf.

## Event Postponement or Rescheduling

If an event is postponed or rescheduled:
- Your existing ticket will remain valid for the new date or venue
- Veent Tix will send you an email notification once the organizer finalizes the updated details
- You do not need to purchase a new ticket
- Unless otherwise stated by the Organizer, your ticket transfers to the new date automatically

## Event Cancellation

If an event is fully canceled:
- The event organizer is responsible for handling any refund requests or alternative solutions
- Alternatives may include event credits, rescheduled access, or partial refunds — at the organizer's discretion
- Veent Tix serves only as a ticketing service provider and does not control event decisions or refund timelines
- Veent Tix shall not be held liable for refunds or damages resulting from event cancellations

## Force Majeure

Neither Veent nor the Organizer shall be liable for delays or failures in performance caused by:
- Acts of nature (earthquakes, floods, typhoons)
- Public emergencies
- Acts of terrorism
- Government restrictions or mandates
- Pandemics
- Labor disputes
- Safety concerns beyond reasonable control

Under such circumstances, no refunds will be issued, and affected parties are deemed released from their contractual obligations.

## Ticket Reselling and Exchanges

Veent Tix prohibits the reselling, duplicating, or counterfeiting of tickets. If you cannot attend an event, Veent Tix does not facilitate ticket transfers or exchanges between users. Contact the event organizer directly for any accommodation requests.

## Disputes

Any disputes arising from event delays, cancellations, or unsatisfactory experiences should be addressed directly between the ticket holder and the event organizer. Veent Tix is not responsible for resolving disputes related to event quality, content, or fulfillment.
